Rocco, Street Chef

Legendary Creature — Elf Druid

At the beginning of your end step, each player exiles the top card of their library.
Until your next end step, each player may play the card they exiled this way.

Whenever a player plays a land from exile or casts a spell from exile,
you put a +1/+1 counter on target creature and create a Food token. (It’s an artifact with “{2}, {T}, Sacrifice this artifact: You gain 3 life.”)
