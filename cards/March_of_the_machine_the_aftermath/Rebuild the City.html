Rebuild the City

Sorcery

Choose target land. Create three tokens that are copies of it,
except they’re 3/3 creatures in addition to their other types and they have vigilance and menace.
(They’re affected by summoning sickness.)

The Phyrexian design had to go, but the materials could be put to good use.
